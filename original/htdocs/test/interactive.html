Interactive Physics

Welcome to the Interactive Physics section. Here you will find interactive tutorials to guide you through some basic physics concepts leading to the physics of plasmas and fusion

Mass, Volume, and Density

In this module, you will explore two basic properties of matter: all matter has mass and it ocupies space. These two properties are combined to form an intrinsic characteristic, that of density.

Virtual Density Lab

Having learned about mass and volume in the previous module, perform a virtual experiment exploring the behaviour of different materials in a variety of liquids.

Electricity and Magnetism

Electric and magnetic forces are dominant factors in many physical systems, particularly in plasma and fusion. This module will introduce these two concepts and explain the connection between the two.

States of Matter

In this module you'll explore the states of matter, particularly the fourth state of matter: plasma. More than 99% of the visible universe is plasma, including all of the stars.

Energy

Energy is not only an important physical concept, but it is of utmost importance for humanity and civilization. In this module we will explore the concept on energy from a physics perspective as well as discuss different energy sources on earth and the struggle to find viable alternative energy sources.

Fusion

Having discussed the importance of green energies, we explain fusion, the energy in the sun and stars. In this final section we discuss the energy production in the center of stars and how we are working on reproducing this process here on earth.
